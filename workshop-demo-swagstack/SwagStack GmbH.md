# SwagStack - Company Context

## Company Overview

**SwagStack GmbH** is a Berlin-based e-commerce company specializing in premium merchandise for the tech industry. We create and distribute high-quality swag for developers, product managers, designers, and tech enthusiasts—while also providing white-label merchandise solutions for tech companies worldwide.

### Mission Statement
*"Empowering tech culture through merchandise that developers actually want to wear."*

### Vision
To become the #1 destination for tech merchandise in Europe, serving both individual tech professionals and companies looking to build authentic brand culture through quality swag.

---

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

| Stream | % of Revenue | Description |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **B2B Enterprise** | 52% | Custom branded merchandise for tech companies (bulk orders, company stores) |
| **B2C Direct** | 35% | Individual customers buying from swagstack.io |
| **B2B SMB** | 10% | Self-service merch stores for startups and small teams |
| **Marketplace** | 3% | Third-party designer merchandise (revenue share) |

### Key Products

**For Companies (B2B)**
- Custom branded hoodies, t-shirts, accessories
- White-label company swag stores
- Event merchandise packages (conferences, hackathons)
- New hire welcome kits
- Sustainable/eco-friendly merchandise lines

**For Individuals (B2C)**
- Developer-themed apparel ("I ship on Fridays", "PM life", etc.)
- Tech stickers and accessories
- Conference-style merchandise
- Limited edition drops with tech influencers

## Technology Stack

### Platform
- **Frontend**: Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: Node.js, PostgreSQL, Redis
- **Infrastructure**: AWS (EKS, RDS, S3, CloudFront)
- **E-commerce**: Custom-built on Medusa.js
- **Payments**: Stripe, Klarna (BNPL)
- **Analytics**: Amplitude, Mixpanel, Metabase

### Integrations
- Shopify (for white-label stores)
- Slack (B2B ordering bot)
- Figma (design-to-merch pipeline)
- SAP (enterprise customers)
- Various print-on-demand partners

## Key Challenges (Current)

1. **Scaling B2B Operations**: Enterprise customers demand faster turnaround and more customization options
2. **International Expansion**: High demand from UK and Nordics but fulfillment costs are eating margins
3. **Platform Reliability**: Recent incidents during Black Friday highlighted infrastructure gaps
4. **Sustainability Goals**: Customers increasingly asking for eco-friendly options, supply chain not fully ready
5. **Competition**: New entrants like Printful and Gooten adding enterprise features

---

## Strategic Priorities (2025-2026)

1. **Enterprise Growth**: Double B2B ARR through improved self-service and enterprise features
2. **International Expansion**: Launch UK fulfillment center, expand Nordic presence
3. **Platform Maturity**: Achieve 99.9% uptime, reduce page load times by 40%
4. **Sustainability**: 50% of catalog to be eco-certified by end of 2026
5. **Marketplace Growth**: 10x third-party designer revenue through creator program
